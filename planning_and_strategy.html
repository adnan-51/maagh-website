Media Planning Process

The fundamental purpose of a media plan is to determine the best way to convey a message to the target audience. Maagh has a systematic set of process that synchronizes all contributing elements in order to achieve a specific goal. It can be broken down into 4 stages –

  • Media planning strategies with Market & competition analysis.
  • Establishment of objectives for Media planning.
  • Strategy development & implementation for media planning.
  • Media planning solutions with campaign evaluations, follow-up & improvements.

You Should Know

Media planning strategies involve selecting the best media platforms to deliver marketing messages effectively. Incorporating market and competition analysis.

Establishing objectives for media planning involves setting clear goals to guide the campaign strategy, such as increasing brand awareness, driving traffic, generating leads, and maximizing ROI.

Strategy development and implementation for media planning involve selecting media channels, scheduling, budgeting, and executing a plan to achieve marketing objectives effectively.
